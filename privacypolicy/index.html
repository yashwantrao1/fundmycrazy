Fund My Crazy

Privacy & Data Protection

10.1 Information Collection

By participating, you consent to Google collecting and processing:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, address, date of birth)
  • Educational information (college, course, year of study)
  • Submission materials (idea description, mockup, supporting documents)
  • Communications with Google during the Campaign
  • Event participation data (attendance, photos, videos)

10.2 Use of Information

Google will use your information to:

  • Administer the Campaign and evaluate submissions
  • Verify eligibility and contact winners
  • Process prize payments
  • Promote the Campaign and winners
  • Improve future campaigns and products

10.3 Data Sharing

Your information may be shared with:

  • Judging panel members (for evaluation purposes)
  • Third-party service providers assisting with Campaign administration
  • Tax authorities (as required by law)
  • The public (name, college, idea title/description for winner announcements)

10.4 Data Retention

Google will retain your information for:

  • Duration of Campaign and prize fulfilment
  • As long as necessary for promotional purposes
  • As required by applicable laws and regulations

10.5 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for promotional use of your information (though this may affect prize eligibility)

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@fundmycrazy.com.

10.6 Privacy Policy

Your participation is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy available at Privacy Policy. In case of conflict between these Terms and the Privacy Policy, these Terms prevail for Campaign-specific matters.
