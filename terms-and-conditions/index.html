Stage 2: Judging Panel
Qualifying submissions will be evaluated by a panel of judges selected by Google based on the following criteria:

Criteria Weight Description
Campus Impact & Need 30% Number of students affected; genuine demand for solution
Feasibility & Execution 25% Realistic implementation; participant capability
Originality & Cool Factor 20% Fresh approach; student desirability
Pitch Quality 15% Quality of Gemini mockup; clarity of articulation
Fun & Joy 10% Enhancement of campus life enjoyment
